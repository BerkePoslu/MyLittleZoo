What You Can Expect To Find

Igaf

Igaf, also known as Igel-Schaf (Hedgehog-sheep in English), is a hybrid animal that originated in Germany. It is a cross between a hedgehog (Igel) and a sheep (Schaf). The Igaf has the body and wool of a sheep, but with the spines of a hedgehog. This unique combination makes it a fascinating and unusual creature. While Igafs do not occur naturally, they have been bred in captivity for their novelty and entertainment value. Despite their unusual appearance, Igafs are not recommended as pets, as they require specialized care and diet, and may not thrive in domestic settings.

Cygama

Cygama or cygne-lama, a remarkable hybrid animal that is said to originate from Switzerland, is the result of an unexpected encounter between a swan and a llama. While the exact circumstances of this unlikely pairing are shrouded in mystery, locals believe it to be the work of a powerful sorcerer who sought to create a creature of great beauty and grace. Despite its enchanting appearance, the Swama is a shy and elusive creature, preferring to dwell in secluded places where it can bask in the beauty of its surroundings undisturbed.

Whaluin

Whaluin is a fascinating hybrid animal that has recently been discovered in the icy waters surrounding Antarctica. This unique creature is the result of a rare genetic mutation that occurred in a group of emperor penguins, causing them to develop whale-like features. This remarkable creature is a social animal and is often seen swimming in groups or frolicking on the ice with other penguins. Its diet consists mainly of krill and other small marine organisms, which it filters through baleen plates in its mouth, much like its whale counterparts.

About

HybridZoo is a creative and imaginative project that depicts a fictional zoo located in Lausanne, Switzerland. The project is unique in that it features hybrid animals that are created by blending two different species. This open source school project was developed as a fun and educational way to explore genetics and the possibilities that arise when combining different animal traits.
